# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

adaptive

## Users

InternNotifs is for students who need to keep up with technical internship postings while managing a high-volume application process. They check the app in short gaps between classes and other commitments, and need a clear view of what changed without turning internship search into another system to maintain.

## Product Purpose

InternNotifs is a lightweight, open-source mobile internship radar. It helps students stay confidently up to date with credible technical internship postings, then takes them quickly to the employer's official application form. Success is a student feeling reliably informed without repeatedly refreshing a GitHub repository or managing a complicated application workflow.

## Positioning

The simple, iPhone-native way to stay current on credible internship postings without the complexity of a job board or application CRM.

## Brand Personality

Simple, efficient, and fun. The experience is calm and task-first, with small moments of reward around genuinely new opportunities rather than attention-seeking decoration across every screen.

## Anti-references

Do not make InternNotifs feel like a complex application CRM.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the student informed, not busy: the product should make change and relevance obvious in seconds.
2. Prefer a short, clear path to the official employer form over expanding tracking or workflow management.
3. Make native behavior feel at home on each platform while preserving one recognizable product voice.
4. Use personality sparingly: a little reward for new opportunities, never ambient noise.
5. Keep the experience lightweight enough to replace manual repository-refresh habits.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Honor system Reduce Motion preferences. Follow platform-native accessibility behavior and minimum touch-target guidance on iOS and Android.
