InternNotifs Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

InternNotifs helps people discover internships, track applications, and choose notification preferences. This policy describes the data used by the beta app.

Data we process

When you create an account, we process your email address and authentication information through Amazon Cognito. If you choose to use application-profile features, we process the profile information, links, education, reusable answers, application-tracking records, and résumé or other documents you upload. We also process your job-alert filters, notification wording preferences, and Expo push token when you enable notifications.

Why we use it

We use this data to provide sign-in, show and filter internships, send requested alerts, keep your profile and application tracker available across sessions, and operate and secure the service. We do not sell personal information or use it for advertising.

Service providers

InternNotifs uses Amazon Web Services for authentication, data storage, API delivery, and encrypted document storage. It uses Expo’s push service to deliver alerts to Apple Push Notification service or Firebase Cloud Messaging. These providers process data only as needed to provide their services.

Retention and deletion

You can delete your account in the app at Profile → Delete account. Deletion removes your profile, applications, uploaded documents, saved device-alert records, and Cognito sign-in account from the active service. Backup retention may be limited by the operational retention settings of the underlying providers.

Your choices

You can browse internships without an account. You can change alert filters and notification wording in Profile, turn alerts off, and deny or revoke notification permission in your device settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, account-deletion help, or a copy of your information, use the InternNotifs support page.
