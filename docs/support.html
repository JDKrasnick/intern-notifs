InternNotifs Support

For beta feedback, technical support, privacy questions, or account help, open a support issue on GitHub. Do not include passwords, government identifiers, or résumé documents in a public issue.

Delete your account

In the app, open Profile and choose Delete account, then confirm. This deletes your profile, saved applications, uploaded documents, device-alert records, and sign-in account.

If you cannot access the app, open a support issue with the email address used for the account and the subject “Account deletion request.” We may request limited information to verify ownership before deleting an inaccessible account.
